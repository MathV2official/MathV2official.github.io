How to play

Controls: WASD to move, Mouse to aim and shoot. Right-Click or Space to jump

This is an aerial, fast-paced and fun first-person shooting game. Make amazing tricks like air-kill or long shot to score more points. Use jumpad or rocket jumps to evade your opponents in the arena.

Walking around
Use W A S D keys to move around

Looking around

Customize sensitivity

You can change the mouse sensitivity and other preferences from the settings.

Press ESC and click on the   gear icon.

✔Check out the settings screen
Jump
Use SPACE or RMB (right mouse button) to jump
✔Perform a jump
Jump-pads

Jump-pads make you leap high up in the air.

 

Upward jump-pads display the jump direction with flowing white rings


 

Forward jump-pads display the jump direction with moving arrows

✔Use a jump-pad
Teleport
 

When you walk into the teleport gate you will exit from another teleport gate.

You can go back and forth between associated teleport gates.

✔Enter a teleport gate
Nailgun

Press LMB (left mouse button) to shoot

Nailgun characteristics

 
  • Long range
  • No spread or recoil
  • High rate of fire
  • Low damage

💡 TIP: don't stick with the Nailgun, always look for a better weapon as soon as you spawn. Use the Nailgun to finish off opponents from a distance.

✔Eliminate a target with the Nailgun
Changing weapons

You can change weapons using keys 1 2 3... 8 or Mouse Wheel

💡 TIP: To reassign weapon keys press ESC, click on the   gear icon and scroll down to the Weapons section.
It's better to reassign weapon keys to be easier to reach.
Weapons shortcuts are displayed on the right
✔Try switching weapons
Shotgun

Press 3 to switch to Shotgun

Shotgun characteristics

 
  • High damage at close range
  • Low damage from afar
  • Shoots several bullets spreading in a cone
✔Eliminate a target with the Shotgun
Rocket Launcher

Press 2 to switch to Rocket Launcher

Rocket Launcher characteristics

 
  • Direct hit causes high damage
  • Splash damage within blast range
  • No self-damage
  • Blast pushes away players
✔Eliminate a target with the Rocket Launcher
Flamethrower

Press 7 to switch to Flamethrower

Flamethrower characteristics

 
  • Fire sticks to surfaces and causes continuous damage
  • Causes self damage
  • Provides thrust while airborne
✔Eliminate a target with the Flamethrower
Killer Cactus

Press 8 to switch to Killer Cactus

Killer Cactus characteristics

 
  • One hit eliminates the opponent
✔Eliminate a target with the Killer Cactus
Grenade Launcher

Press 4 to switch to Grenade Launcher

Grenade Launcher characteristics

 
  • High damage on direct hit
  • Splash damage within blast range
  • No self-damage
  • Grenades explode on contact or after a certain time
✔Eliminate a target with the Grenade Launcher
Lifegun

Eliminate a target with the Lifegun

Press 6 to switch to Lifegun

Lifegun characteristics

 
  • Limited range
  • Steals opponent's health and replenishes yours

💡Get within range before trying to lock on targets. They will be locked while in range with no obstacles in-between. You can lock multiple targets at once.

✔Eliminate a target with the Lifegun
Railgun

Press 6 to switch to Railgun

Railgun characteristics

 
  • Long range
  • Slow rate of fire
  • High damage
  • Pushes opponents away
✔Eliminate a target with the Railgun
Magic Milk
 
  • Triples the damage you deal
  • Spawns periodically in one or more places
  • Effects last for a brief time
  • Is dropped if the player is killed
💡 TIP: You may have to perform a ramp-jump to get to the Magic Milk
✔Get the Magic Milk and eliminate a target
