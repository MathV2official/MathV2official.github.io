How to Play

This is how to play swordbattle.io

Movement


Moving:

To move around the board, use the arrow keys or the W A S D keys


Direction:

To change the direction that you're moving in, move the mouse around

Attacking


Hitting:

To attack another player, left click the mouse on that player. If they are in range, then you will damage them. You should see their health bar go down.


Killing:

When you kill a player, they will disappear. They'll drop all of their coins which you can then pick up and use to level yourself up. Your kill counter in the top left will also increase by one.

Levels


Coins:

You can collect coins by travelling around the map and bumping into them. You also get coins from opponents that have died. Coins are used to help you level up.


Levels:

When you collect enough coins, you'll level up. At the bottom of your screen is a bar that shows how far you are to the next level.
