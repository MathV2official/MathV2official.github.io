Hints for mastering Little Alchemy 2

  • Air, fire, earth and water will lead you to countless discoveries.
  • Try mixing items with a copy of themselves — wall + wall will surely result in a house.
  • Some items like human or plant will lead to more discoveries than others.
  • There is no penalty for exploring. Try any combination that comes to your mind.
  • Remember that only two items can be mixed at a time.
  • Some items can’t be discovered by mixing. You will unlock them when certain progress is reached.
  • Try with science in mind! While simplified, most combinations mirror the real world.
  • Some items don’t mix with anything else. Hide them with a tap to keep the focus on what’s important!
  • When stuck, try the official Little Alchemy 2 cheats.
  • The official hints page lists all items combinations. Take a peek, maybe you’ll find just what you need to keep going.
  • While using our iOS and Android apps you can get hints crafted just for you.
